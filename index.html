Kyoko was created from a special implant blood, the blood of the ancient mechanical phoenix. The phoenix's blood was so hot that it could fuse two worlds together. Kae sacrificed two universes to create Kyoko.

Kyoko has an extremely contrary personality and doesn't get along well with anyone. She harbored a deep hatred for her creator, Kae, and thus had a special hatred for him, until Rai NightWalker found her...
